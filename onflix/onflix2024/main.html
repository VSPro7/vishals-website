Onflix 2024

Onflix is an organization that organizes sporting events since 2020. There are many sporting events like marathon, walkathon, badminton, football, cricket, discus throw, javlin throw, shooting, athletics, chess, air hockey, obsticle cource and so on.

The organization was founded by Vishal Shetty in the year of 2020. During the pandemic of covid-19 he was not allowed to go out of the house due to the lockdowns. So, one day he decided to start a sporting event in which everyone could participate and play, and so Onflix was created.

So, if you are interested click here to register.

RULES

  • Each team gets 1 point for participation in every sport.
  • The winning team of each sport gets 10 points.
  • The runner up of each sport gets 5 points.
  • In the end the top three teams on the leaderboard get their respective awards.
